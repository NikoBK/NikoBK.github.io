About Me

Hi! I am a student at Aalborg University (AAU) who is finishing up their bachelor of science (b.sc) in Robotics Engineering. I am currently looking forward to beginning a master of science (m.sc) in biomedical engineering with the hopes of combining both degrees to be a biomedical robotics engineer who specializes in robotics applied in the health sector worldwide.
