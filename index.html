About Me

I am an engineer from Denmark who has studied robotics and biomedicine. I aspire to work in a field where technology can be merged with the human body and mind. I also invest time and energy in studying psychology, and some of my pastime hobbies are arts and crafts, game development, and playing guitar. I have created this webpage as an outlet for many of these interests of mine to share with those of you who are reading this (but also those who stopped reading a few words ago).

Some of the people who have inspired me the most to make this blog are: Amit Partel, Diba Alam, Mads Mogensen, Sebastian Lague, and ThinMatrix. If any of you ever read this, I am honored to have you here. This goes for everyone in general, so to all of you reading, whoever you are, thank you for visiting. I hope you enjoy your stay!

The blog page shows all blog posts I have published that may be tutorials, guides, thoughts on my mind, etc., while my portfolio page shows projects that explore and define my skills on a larger and more technical scope than my pastime interests.
